critical Buttons must have discernible text

Ensure buttons have discernible text

Who this locks out: Screen-reader and voice-control users cannot tell what these buttons or links do.

Scope and limitations

This is an automated snapshot, not a certification. Automated tools reliably detect only a portion (typically 30–40%) of WCAG 2.1 AA success criteria — issues like keyboard traps, focus order, screen-reader task flows, and meaningful alt text require expert manual testing. A clean automated scan does not mean a site is EAA-compliant; a full audit against EN 301 549 is the recognised path. This document is provided for information purposes and is not legal advice.
