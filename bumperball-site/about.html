What Is Bumper Ball?

Soccer, But You're The Ball

Bumper Ball Chicago is known for Bubble Soccer — a unique soccer game played on a grassy field or in an indoor facility. Each player climbs inside a giant inflatable bubble that covers them from the waist up, with their feet free to run. The objective? Score goals… while bouncing off everyone in your way.

It's the rare activity that's just as fun to watch as it is to play — every collision sends someone rolling, and everyone comes up laughing.

Beyond classic bubble soccer, we run a full lineup of games: Free-For-All, the Captain's Game, and Sharks & Minnows. Your event coordinator keeps things moving so no two rounds feel the same.
