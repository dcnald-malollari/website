FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your party. Still have a question? Call or write — we're quick to answer.

Does the price include setup and delivery?

Yes — setup and delivery are included in every package. If your location needs power, a generator is a small $10–20 extra fee.

Does the rental time include your setup time?

No. We arrive early to set up, so you get your entire party time to play.

When do you set up?

That depends on how many rentals we have that day. Generally our crew arrives 15 minutes to 1 hour before your rental time begins, so everything is ready at game time.

What about parks? Do parks have electricity?

We love setting up at parks! But most parks do NOT have electricity. If you want to set up at a park and power is needed, you must rent a generator — we rent them at a reasonable cost. Also, park space is first come, first served, so get your spot early in the day.

What payments do you take?

Credit cards or debit cards.

What if we need to cancel?

You'll see our refund policy during checkout. If anything comes up, give us a call at (331) 431-1134 and we'll help you out.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes — all orders require a 50% deposit.

What surfaces do you set up on?

We can set up on grass (our favorite, and best for the kids), dirt, asphalt, and concrete. Sorry, we can't set up on any type of rocks — the constant rubbing wears through the equipment.

How many people can play at once?

Every package includes 10 official bumper ball suits. Players rotate and take water breaks every 5–10 minutes between games, so bigger groups keep everyone in the action.

What ages can play?

We bring adult-size balls for ages 10 and up and kid-size balls for ages 10 and under.

What should we bring?

Just water and comfortable clothing — we bring everything else, including all the game equipment and a coordinator/referee.

Do players need to sign anything?

Yes — every participant signs a quick online waiver before playing (a parent or guardian signs for players under 18). You can read and sign it on our Waiver page.
