Good To Know

Quick Answers

Does the price include setup and delivery?

Yes! Setup and delivery are included. If your location needs power (most parks do), a generator can be added for a small $10–20 fee.

How many people can play?

Every package comes with 10 bumper ball suits. Players rotate and take water breaks every 5–10 minutes between games, so groups of 20+ still keep everyone in the action.

What ages can play?

We bring adult-size balls for ages 10 and up and kid-size balls for ages 10 and under — everyone plays.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, all orders require a 50% deposit. We accept credit and debit cards.
