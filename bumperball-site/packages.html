What To Expect

Your Party, Handled

1

We come to you

You choose the location — backyard, park, gym, or field — and two of our employees arrive 15–30 minutes before your start time to set everything up.

2

We run the whole event

Your coordinator organizes and supervises from start to finish: how to get in the bubbles, safety precautions, and all the games your group wants to play.

3

Everyone gets to play

Players rotate and take water breaks every 5–10 minutes in between games, so everyone gets a chance to bump and bounce.

What to bring

Just bring water and comfortable clothing — we handle the rest.

Playing at a park?

Most parks don't have electricity. If power is needed, we rent generators for a small $10–20 fee. Park space is first come, first served — claim your spot early!

Payments & deposit

All orders require a 50% deposit, payable by credit or debit card. Our refund policy is shown during checkout.
