Privacy Policy

We guarantee that your credit card transactions are safe and secure, and we guarantee the safety of any information you provide us.

What will you do with my personal information?

The owner of Bumperball Chicago is committed to your privacy. Any information you elect to provide us — name, e-mail address, preferences — is kept confidential. We pledge that we will never provide that information to anyone else. We do not share or disclose any mobile information provided by you, including phone numbers, with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

We only use your e-mail address, and any other information you elect to provide, to better serve you and to keep you informed about changes to our site, party and rental ideas, or new products and special pricing available to our customers. Your information is safe with us.

Is it really safe to use my credit card?

Yes. Online reservations are processed on a secure server using industry-standard SSL encryption — the same technology used by major online retailers. Your payment data is encrypted from the moment you submit it.

Based on the Fair Credit Billing Act, your bank cannot hold you liable for more than $50.00 of fraudulent charges. If your bank should exercise its right to hold you liable for that $50.00, Bumperball Chicago will reimburse you up to the full $50.00, provided the unauthorized use of your card resulted through no fault of your own from purchases made with bumperballchicago.com on our SSL secure server. In the event of unauthorized use of your credit card, you must notify your credit card provider immediately in accordance with its reporting rules and procedures.
