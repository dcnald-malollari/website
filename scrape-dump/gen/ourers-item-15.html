The best part of Bumperball Chicago is that we bring the party to any location you would like.
Bumperball Chicago known for Bubble Soccer, is a unique soccer game where participants play in a grassy field or indoor facility. The participants will be inside a plastic bubble covering all the way from the waist up. It is a sport that allows players to run around and collide into each other with their feet being free to move. The objective of Bubble Soccer is to attempt scoring a goal, while being inside the plastic bubble. Also, we have other designed games for Bumperball consisting of Free for all, captain game, sharks and minnows.
 
 
 
 

How It Works:

You choose the location for your party and we have 2 employees come set up 15-30 minutes prior to your start time. They will organize and supervise the whole event from start to finish. This includes them explaining how to get in the bubbles, safety precautions and explaining all the games the group would like to play. 

 

Rotations:

Players will be rotating/taking water breaks every 5-10 minutes in between games so everyone will get a chance to play. 

 

What to bring:

Please bring water and comfortable clothing.
